Mornings feel slower when breakfast is set on these sage linen mats. Stonewashed Belgian linen softens with every wash, developing a beautiful lived-in texture that only gets better over time.

  • Dimensions: 35 × 45 cm per mat
  • Material: 100% Belgian linen, stonewashed finish
  • Set includes: 4 placemats
  • Hemstitched edges, mitered corners
  • Colours: Sage, Terracotta, Stone, Natural Linen
  • Machine wash at 40°C, gentle cycle
  • Do not bleach or tumble dry on high heat
  • Tumble dry low or line dry — linen softens further with each wash
  • Iron slightly damp on medium heat if needed
  • Natural wrinkles are part of the linen character — embrace them
  • Free shipping on orders over €75 · Netherlands & Belgium
  • Standard delivery: 2–4 business days (PostNL)
  • International shipping: 5–8 business days
  • 14-day right of return — EU consumer law
  • Return shipping label provided for defective items

These placemats are made by the Sharma family workshop in Jaipur, India — a three-generation family of textile artisans. We pay 35% above the regional fair wage, visit every year, and produce in small batches to avoid overproduction. Every piece is inspected by hand before shipping.
