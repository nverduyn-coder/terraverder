We reject vague sustainability claims

Words like "eco-friendly" and "conscious" are meaningless without specifics. On this page you'll find exactly what we do, how we verify it and where we still have work to do. Honest accountability matters more to us than good marketing.

GOTS

OT

OEKO-TEX® 100

01 · Materials

What's in your product — and where it comes from

We use only four core materials: Belgian linen, GOTS-certified organic cotton, natural jute and hand-harvested seagrass. Each is chosen for its biodegradability, durability and low environmental impact.

Belgian linen uses 85% less water than conventional cotton
All dyes OEKO-TEX® certified — no azo dyes, no heavy metals
Seagrass is wild-harvested and fully regenerative
No synthetic fibres in any product, ever
0 synthetic fibres
02 · People

The artisans who make everything

We work with 14 artisan family workshops across India and Portugal. We visit every partner at least once per year, pay 35% above regional minimum wage, and do not accept subcontracting without our written approval.

14 partner workshops, all independently Fair Trade audited
35% above regional minimum wage, paid on time
No child labour — zero tolerance, zero exceptions
Annual factory visits by our founder, Emma de Vries
14 families
03 · Packaging

Zero plastic. Not "reduced plastic." Zero.

Every single piece of TerraVerde packaging is plastic-free — from the outer shipping box to the inner tissue wrap. In 2025 we eliminated 418 kg of plastic from our supply chain versus our 2023 baseline.

Recycled cardboard boxes — FSC certified
Tissue paper in brand colours — acid-free, compostable
Paper tape — no plastic adhesive
Printed thank-you card on 100% recycled stock
418 kg plastic avoided
04 · Circularity

Our take-back programme — closing the loop

When your TerraVerde product reaches the end of its life, we want it back. Send it to us and we'll upcycle, compost or donate it — and give you 15% off your next order. No product we've made has gone to landfill.

Take-back label included with every order — prepaid return
Worn items repaired by our artisan partners
Unsalvageable items composted or recycled as industrial textile
15% credit on your next purchase for every return
0 products to landfill
